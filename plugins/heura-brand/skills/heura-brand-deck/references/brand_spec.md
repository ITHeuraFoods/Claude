# Heura 2026 Brand Specification

## Brand Identity

**Company**: Heura (plant-based food company)  
**Archetype**: The Outlaw — bold, rule-breaking, authentic, confident  
**Year**: 2026  

The Heura brand is unapologetic, visually loud, and designed to disrupt. Every visual element should radiate confidence and break conventions.

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Color
- **Heura Yellow**: `#FDE01D`
  - High visibility, immediately recognizable
  - Use in logos, key visual elements, backgrounds
  - Must appear in every deck (even if subtle)

### Secondary Colors
- **Black**: `#000000` — authority, contrast, elegance
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` — clarity, negative space, breathing room

### Tertiary Colors (Graphic Details Only)
These are **never** used as slide backgrounds:
- **Orange**: `#FF7700` — energy, warmth (accent lines, icons, illustrations)
- **Green**: `#00AE44` — growth, plant-based association
- **Light Green**: `#85C300` — freshness, lighter alternative to green

---

## Approved Background Combinations

Use **one of these three universes per slide**:

### Universe 1: Yellow Background
- Background: `#FDE01D` (Heura Yellow)
- Text: Black `#000000` or White `#FFFFFF`
- Accents: Black, white, or tertiary colors
- Feeling: Bold, energetic, confident
- Best for: Key messages, title slides, call-to-action

### Universe 2: Black Background
- Background: `#000000` (Black)
- Text: White `#FFFFFF` or Yellow `#FDE01D`
- Accents: White, yellow, or tertiary colors
- Feeling: Premium, sophisticated, dramatic
- Best for: Deep-dive content, dark/serious topics, contrast

### Universe 3: White Background
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (White)
- Text: Black `#000000`
- Accents: Yellow, black, or tertiary colors
- Feeling: Clean, professional, readable
- Best for: Data-heavy slides, detailed information, accessibility

## Typography

### Font Stack

| Purpose | Primary Font | Weight | Source | Usage Notes |
|---------|--------------|--------|--------|------------|
| **Brand Headings** | Heura | Regular | Custom (`Heura.woff`) — heurafoods.com | Primary heading font used across the website (h1–h3). The official digital brand typeface. |
| **Display / Impact** | Veneer | Regular | Licensed | UPPERCASE ONLY. For major impact moments — opening slides, closing, key statements. |
| **Punchy Headlines** | Druk / Druk Wide | Bold | Licensed | Short, bold statements. "Say it loud." Max 2-3 lines. |
| **Titles & Headers** | Poppins | Semibold, Bold | Google Fonts (free) | Section headers, slide titles, prominent call-outs. Workhorse font. |
| **Body Copy** | Poppins | Medium, Semibold | Google Fonts (free) | Paragraph text, bullet points, explanations. Maximum readability. |
| **Editorial Alternative** | Reckless Condensed | — | Licensed | Used sparingly for stylistic variation. Not for body copy. |
| **Decorative / Special** | Pixel Grafiti | Regular | Custom (`Pixel-Grafiti.woff`) — heurafoods.com | Pixel-style display font for decorative use only. Never for body text or key messages. |

### Font Licensing & Fallbacks

- **Heura**: Custom brand font from heurafoods.com — download from the brand assets repo or `/fonts/heura/Heura.woff`.
- **Pixel Grafiti**: Custom decorative font from heurafoods.com — `/fonts/pixel/Pixel-Grafiti.woff`.
- **Veneer, Druk, Druk Wide, Reckless Condensed**: Licensed fonts — install before building decks.
- **Poppins**: Google Fonts, freely available — fallback for all unlicensed contexts.
- **Fallback hierarchy**: Heura → Veneer/Druk → Poppins. If brand fonts unavailable, use **Poppins** everywhere.

### Typography Rules

1. **Max 3 typefaces per slide**. Typical: Heura or Druk for headline + Poppins for body = 2. Occasionally 3 if adding Veneer or Reckless Condensed.
2. **Veneer is UPPERCASE ONLY.** Never mixed case, never lowercase.
3. **Heura is the primary digital heading font** — matches the heurafoods.com website; use it to maintain brand consistency in digital-first presentations.
4. **Pixel Grafiti is decorative only** — never use for slide titles, body copy, or any content that needs to be legible at small sizes.
5. **Hierarchy**: Large headline (24pt+) → body copy (12-14pt). Contrast them visually.
6. **Line spacing**: 1.2x for headlines, 1.5x for body copy (readability).
7. **Contrast**: Text must be legible against background. Use white on dark, black on light.

## Visual Language

### Layout & Spacing
- **Generous negative space**: Don't fill every inch. Breathing room = confidence.
- **Bold blocks**: Large color blocks (yellow, black) with text layered on top.
- **Alignment**: Left-align body copy, center titles. Be consistent.
- **Margins**: Min 40px from slide edge for text.

### Logo Usage
- Always use the Heura logo on title/closing slides.
- **Never** apply shadows, effects, or distortions to the logo.
- Logo can sit on yellow, black, or white background.
- Minimum clearance: 40px on all sides.

### Imagery & Icons
- Use high-contrast photography (not soft, washed-out images).
- Illustrations should match the bold, confident aesthetic.
- Icons: Use the brand color palette. Make them chunky, readable.
- Plant imagery: Celebrate plant-based food; avoid clichés.

### Graphics & Data Visualization
- **Charts**: Use yellow, black, white as primary. Tertiary colors for accent/detail.
- **Lines**: Bold lines (3-4pt) in yellow or black. Thin lines look weak.
- **Shapes**: Bold geometric shapes (rectangles, circles) with solid colors.
- **No gradients or soft transitions** — they weaken the brand.

## Do's & Don'ts

### ✅ DO

- Use yellow prominently and fearlessly
- Create high-contrast visuals (dark on light, light on dark)
- Go bold with typography — big headlines, punchy copy
- Leave generous negative space
- Use Poppins as the workhorse font
- Apply the Outlaw archetype: confident, rule-breaking, authentic
- Mix yellow and black confidently
- Use white/light backgrounds for readability-heavy content

### ❌ DON'T

- Use light green (`#85C300`) on yellow — unreadable, bad vibes
- Use dark green (`#00AE44`) on black — poor contrast
- Apply shadows, effects, or glossy finishes (looks cheap)
- Distort or modify the Heura logo
- Mix multiple tertiary colors in one slide
- Use more than 3 fonts per slide
- Crowd content — this brand thrives on space and boldness
- Use light, washed-out color palettes (they contradict the Outlaw archetype)
- Overuse yellow as a background (balance with black/white)

---

## Accessibility & Readability

- **Color contrast ratio**: Minimum 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for headlines (WCAG AA).
- **Font size**: Minimum 18pt for body, 24pt+ for headlines.
- **Font weight**: Never go lighter than Medium for body copy.
- **Test readability**: Especially on projectors (yellow on white may fade).

## Examples by Slide Type

### Title Slide
- **Background**: Yellow (`#FDE01D`)
- **Title font**: Veneer (UPPERCASE) or Druk Bold
- **Title color**: Black (`#000000`)
- **Logo**: Center or bottom-right
- **Accent**: Possible black block on right edge

### Content Slide
- **Background**: White or Black (depends on content density)
- **Title**: Poppins Semibold or Druk, 28pt
- **Body**: Poppins Medium, 14pt, left-aligned
- **Accent**: Yellow block or line (left edge, bottom edge)

### Data/Chart Slide
- **Background**: White (for clarity)
- **Chart colors**: Yellow, black, white, with tertiary accents
- **Title**: Poppins Bold, 24pt
- **Legend**: Small, Poppins Medium, 12pt

### Closing/Call-to-Action Slide
- **Background**: Black (`#000000`)
- **CTA text**: Veneer UPPERCASE or Druk Bold, white or yellow
- **Accent**: Yellow block behind or beside text
- **Logo**: Bottom-right corner

- **Primary**: .pptx (Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Slides compatible)
- **Fallback fonts**: Always embed fonts or use system-safe alternatives
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (16:9 widescreen standard)
